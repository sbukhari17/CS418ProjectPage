Query 6: Sentiment Counts vs Ratings for Restaurants

Overview

For Query 6 I used the results from Query 5 (sentiment for each review w/ rating) and counted the occurences of sentiments to find the most frequent sentiment. I then combined the most frequent sentiment for a given restaurant with its average rating.
