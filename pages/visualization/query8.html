Query 8: Viability of a Business

Overview

For Query 8, the task was to find businesses that went out of business after failing a food inspection. I extracted information from the Food Inspections data set from the City of Chicago data portal. For these specific businesses, I calculated the difference between the two dates two get the amount of years that the business was active after the failed inspection.
